Aerial Robotic

A group of students including me, were incharge of designing a swarm network using the X-bee module between multiple quadcopters. The X-bee module has the capability of changing connectivity port dynamically, however it is not instantaniouly. This is a huge problem as the Quadcopters will lose connectivity with the swarm and becomes 'blind' for a brief moment.
